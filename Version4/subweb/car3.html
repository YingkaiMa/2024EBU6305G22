2002 Nissan Skyline GT-R R34
2024-12-02   admin   548

The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is an iconic Japanese sports sedan known for its outstanding performance and handling. In the Speed and Fury series, Brian O'Connor has driven this car multiple times, demonstrating its fast acceleration and excellent handling. This car is equipped with an RB26DETT inline six cylinder turbocharged engine, providing strong power output and excellent driving performance. In the film, Brian's Skyline GT-R R34 underwent a series of modifications, including the addition of a larger turbocharger, upgraded exhaust system, and customized exterior kits, making it a high-performance racing car.

Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution VII
2024-12-02   admin   548

This car is one of the iconic vehicles of Roman Pierce, making its debut in the second installment of "2 Fast 2 Furious". It is a 2002 Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution VII, a Japanese sports sedan with excellent performance and handling. Roman's Lancer Evolution VII has undergone a series of modifications, including the addition of larger turbochargers, adjusted suspension systems, and personalized exterior kits, making it a highly competitive racing car.

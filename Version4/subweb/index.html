The Fast & Furious is a race-themed action crime film directed by Rob Cohen and starring Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Michelle Rodriguez, Jordana Brewster, Tyrese Gibson, Luda Criss, Cheng Kang, Dwayne Johnson and Jason Statham from 2001 to 2021. Nine films were made.

The film is packed with adrenaline-fueled racing sequences, intense action, and a gripping storyline that keeps audiences on the edge of their seats. With its dynamic cast, thrilling stunts, and a killer soundtrack, "The Fast and the Furious" is a high-speed thrill ride that leaves viewers craving more.The final sequel, "Fast and Furious 10," will be released in 2023

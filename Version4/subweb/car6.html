Dodge Charger R/T
2024-12-02   admin   548

This car is the iconic vehicle of Dominic Toretto, appearing in multiple movies in the Speed and Fury series. Its brand is Dodge, and the model is Charger R/T. It is a classic model with a strong American Muscle Car style. This car first appeared in the first film "The Fast and the Furious" and became a symbol and iconic racing car for Doni. The Charger R/T has undergone multiple modifications, including the addition of a more powerful engine, improved suspension system, and customized exterior kits, making it a highly performance racing car.

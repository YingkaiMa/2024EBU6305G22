Mazda RX-7 of Han
2024-12-02   admin   548

The Mazda RX-7 is a Japanese sports sedan praised for its lightweight body and excellent handling. In "Speed and Fury 3: Tokyo Drift," Han Lue drove this car and demonstrated his driving skills in drift races. This car is equipped with a Wankel type rotary engine, providing strong power output and flexible handling at high speeds. The Han RX-7 has undergone a series of modifications, including the addition of larger turbochargers, adjusted suspension systems, and lightweight body components, making it perform well in drift races.
